Destination

Virtual Brand
Experience

A virtual brand filled with people accustomed to treating their sweet tooth with 5 star rating delicacies.

Brand Origins

Sweet Treat was made in Summer 2023. Its brand characters (mascots) introduce a fox and a bunny. The fox is colored amber and creamy cinnamon, and the bunny is colored vanilla and cookie dough.

Japanese Candy
Aesthetic

Our aesthetic is inspired by Japanese candy, especially the small chewing gums that came with cute, popular gag-style collectible stickers.

Mystery Collectibles

If you place an order with us, you might get the surprise of seeing a toy collectible, we hid inside as a mystery piece.
